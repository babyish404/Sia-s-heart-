The First Poem I Wrote For You 🤍

I was on my own, enjoying the quiet life of solitude.

I swore to myself I'd never give my heart out again…

Then you came along, and suddenly, solitude sounded like something I wouldn't mind leaving behind.

You somehow disarmed me before I even knew it, and when I finally did, it was already too late—I was yours.

You painted my black-and-white world with colors I knew existed, but never bothered to explore.

The world had always been there… but you made me want to live in it fully.

You became the reason behind my smile, the calm in my chaotic mind, my sun on a rainy day, my muse, an absolute work of art.

You're my quiet place in a noisy world, my favorite thought before sleep, my heartbeat in the calm, my laughter on a gloomy day, my everything I didn't know I needed, my safe place, my home, my peace, my favorite chapter in the story of me.

And though this love came quietly, without warning, it has grown into the happiest part of me, soft, deep, and endless—like a song I never want to stop singing.

With you, I've found the colors, the warmth, the light, and I wouldn't trade a single moment of this for anything in the world.

This is the first poem I ever wrote for you, and I mean every single word of it.
